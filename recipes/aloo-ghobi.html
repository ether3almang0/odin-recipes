Chana Chawal

Description

Aloo Ghobi is a delicious dish based in the north western part of the Indian subcontinent. It is comprised of mainly potato and cauliflower, as per the name. Pairing with roti or chapati, aloo ghobi's veggie packed spice bomb makes for a satisfying lunch to get you through the day.

Like any good Pakistani dish, we first begin with sauteeing onions until golden brown, giving the cauliflower a dark orange hue color. Adrak Lasan and mirchi enhance flavor, while the tomato simmers it down to an enticing sauce that the potato and cauliflower will be cooked in.

Ingredients

  • cauliflower
  • potato
  • onions
  • tomato
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • chillies
  • cilantro
  • red chilli flakes
  • salt
  • cumin powder
  • garam masala
  • red chilli powder

Steps

  1. Chop onion
  2. Glaze pan with olive oil
  3. Add salt
  4. Sautee onion till golden brown
  5. Mince garlic and ginger
  6. Once onions are golden brown add minced garlic and ginger
  7. Add two chillies to the pan
  8. Add 1 teaspoon of red chilli flakes
  9. Add 1 teaspoon of red chilli powder
  10. Once there is an aroma, dice the tomato and add it to the pan
  11. Let the tomato simmer till it is liquid
  12. Chop the cauliflower and add it to the pan
  13. Add 2 teaspoons of cumin powder
  14. Add 1 teaspoon of garam masala
  15. Peel and chop the potatoes and add them to the pan
  16. Cook until aromatic
  17. Add chopped cilantro
  18. Cover the pan for 5-10 mins while it cooks on medium heat
  19. Stir the pan, put a knife through the potato chunks
  20. Repeat the two previous steps until the knife can smoothly go in and out of the potatoes
  21. Voila! A salan to go with a nice, flaky chapati!
