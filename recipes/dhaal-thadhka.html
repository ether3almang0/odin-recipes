Dhaal Thadka

Description

Dhaal Thadka is a delicious dish with variations all around the Indian subcontinent. It is comprised of mainly yellow lentils and fried aromatics, as per the name. High in protein, comforting and savory, this dish is perfect for a cold night-in

Ingredients

  • yellow lentils
  • onions
  • tomato
  • garlic
  • chillies
  • cilantro
  • red chilli flakes
  • salt
  • cumin powder
  • ghee
  • red chili powder

Steps

  1. Soak 1/2 cup of lentils in water over night
  2. Chop onion
  3. Mince garlic
  4. Glaze pan with ghee
  5. Add salt, garlic, chillies and onion to the pan
  6. Sautee components till golden brown
  7. Add whole cumin
  8. Cook till aromatic
  9. In another pot, heat twice the amount of lentils for water to boil
  10. Once boiling, add lentils
  11. Add chopped tomato to pot of lentils
  12. Add red chilli pwder and red chilli flakes to the pot of lentis
  13. Cover with lid and cook on medium flame until aromatic
  14. Add the sauteed components to the pot of lentils
  15. Add chopped cilantro for garnish
  16. Shabash! A yummy pot of lentils to eat with rice!
