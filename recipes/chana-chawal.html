Chana Chawal

Description

Chana Chawal is a delicious dish based in the north western part of the Indian subcontinent. It is comprised of mainly rice and chickpeas, as per the name. Packed with protein, carbs, warmth, and delectably aromatic spices, chana chawal makes for a wonderful dinner with family and friends

Like any good Pakistani dish, we first begin with sauteeing onions until golden brown, giving the basmati rice it's brownish color. Adrak Lasan and mirchi enhance flavor, while the tomato simmers it down to an enticing stew the rice and chickpeas will be cooked in.

Ingredients

  • chickpeas
  • basmati rice
  • onions
  • tomato
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • chillies
  • cilantro
  • red chilli flakes
  • salt
  • cumin powder
  • garam masala
  • chana masala

Steps

  1. Soak 1 1/2 cup of rice in water for half hour
  2. Chop onion
  3. Glaze pan with olive oil
  4. Add salt
  5. Sautee onion till golden brown
  6. Mince garlic and ginger
  7. Once onions are golden brown add minced garlic and ginger
  8. Add two chillies to the pan
  9. Add 1 teaspoon of red chilli flakes
  10. Once there is an aroma, dice the tomato and add it to the pan
  11. Let the tomato simmer till it is liquid
  12. Add chickpeas
  13. Add 2 teaspoons of cumin powder
  14. Add 1 teaspoon of garam masala
  15. Add 1/2 tablespoon of chana masala
  16. Cook until aromatic
  17. Add chopped cilantro
  18. Add two times the amount of rice for water to the pan
  19. Raise the heat and wait for it to boil
  20. Once boiling, add the soaked rice and wait for it to boil again
  21. Once boiling, set heat to low and cover the pan for 15 minutes
  22. Voila! Yummy rice!
